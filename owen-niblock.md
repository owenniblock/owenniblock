# Owen Niblock

## Biography

Owen Niblock is a Senior Software Engineer at GitHub, currently part of the Issues team. He has previously worked in various roles, including 3 years in GitHub's Accessibility Engineering team, 2 years working on npm, and 2 years working on MS App Center Test. Owen started as a C# developer specializing in Sitecore CMS websites before transitioning to Ruby/Rails development at App Center. He later became a node developer at npm before moving back to Rails & React on the Accessibility Team. Owen is known for his versatility and expertise in accessibility.
